# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

Morgames is committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and collaborative environment for everyone.

We expect all contributors to help create a positive community.


## Expected Behavior

Community members should:

- Communicate respectfully.
- Accept constructive feedback.
- Collaborate openly.
- Respect different opinions and approaches.
- Focus on improving the project.


## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not tolerated:

- Harassment or discrimination.
- Personal attacks.
- Intentional spam.
- Malicious actions against the project.
- Misuse of the Morgames brand.


## Enforcement

Violations may result in:

- Warnings.
- Removal of comments or discussions.
- Restrictions from contributing.
- Removal from project spaces.


Our goal is to build Morgames together in a respectful environment.
